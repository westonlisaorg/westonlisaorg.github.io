God's Will Revealed

April 4, 2022 by Weston in Pray

This week my heart has been particularly pressed to pray and seek after God's will in the coming term on the field. It is so easy to make assumptions that we know God's mind.

Please pray:

  • God would increase our desire to see his will done alone.
  • God would change our hearts so that we increasingly desire his glory to be made known.
  • God would reveal his will to us as we return to the field.

Weston and Lisa have been serving in Northeast Thailand for the past eight years among the Isaan people. They are currently leading a team engaged in church planting in the rural countryside.
