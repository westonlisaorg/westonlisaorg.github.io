Josh & Mindy Debrief

March 14, 2022 by Weston in Pray

Next week, on the 20th and 22nd, I will be doing a debrief time with Josh and Mindy, our teammates who are currently on the field. They haved completed their first year in Blackriver province. This is my first time doing a debrief during a term but I thought it would be good to debrief together since the first year is such a crucial period. (Usually debriefs are conducted at the conclusion of a term of service.)

Debrief sessions are very special to me as a team leader. They are opportunities to listen, pray and encourage. I always see God speak to members as they seek him and celebrate him faithfulness.

Please pray:

  • God would lead me as I prepare the activities, materials and schedule.
  • God would speak to Josh and Mindy as they reflect and seek him.
  • Josh and Mindy would be refreshed and encouraged during the debrief on March 20, 22.

Weston and Lisa have been serving in Northeast Thailand for the past eight years among the Isaan people. They are currently leading a team engaged in church planting in the rural countryside.
