Packing Up In Chicago

February 7, 2022 by Weston in Pray

This week we are packing up our house in Chicago. We have lived here for exactly one year. What a refuge and a blessing this house has been. It has been a house of healing for Lisa's Dad. It has been a refuge for us. God has met us and taught us many things in these walls. He has supplied all of our needs. We praise the Lord for this place.

It will be emotional packing up and saying goodbye. This is one of the hard things about this kind of work: we have no permanent place to live.

Please pray that we would feel these transitions deeply.

Weston and Lisa have been serving in Northeast Thailand for the past eight years among the Isaan people. They are currently leading a team engaged in church planting in the rural countryside.
