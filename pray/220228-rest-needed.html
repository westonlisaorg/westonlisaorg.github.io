Rest Needed

February 28, 2022 by Weston

This week we are connecting with supporters in Phoenix and preparing a few other presentations for the coming weeks. The missions conference was incredibly inspiring and encouraging but we are pretty tired.

Please pray:

  • That we would be able to rest and recover from the mission conference and the two presentations we gave last week. We are tired.
  • That God would continue to show us how to prepare upcoming presentations and adapt our material to be inspiring to the various audiences we have been invited to speak with.

Weston and Lisa have been serving in Southeath Asia for the past seven years. They are currently leading a team engaged in village church planting.
