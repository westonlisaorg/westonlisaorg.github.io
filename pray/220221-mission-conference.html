Mission Conference

February 21, 2022 by Weston

This week we are participating in a missions conference at one of our supporting churches in the Phoenix area. We will be presenting on Tuesday, the 22nd.

Please pray:

  • God would give us passion and clarity as we share with many new people about our mission work.
  • That many would be inspired to partner through prayer.
  • That Jesus would be glorified and exalted as we share and meet with many.
  • Our presentation on the 22nd would be smooth and clear.

Weston and Lisa have been serving in Southeath Asia for the past seven years. They are currently leading a team engaged in village church planting.
