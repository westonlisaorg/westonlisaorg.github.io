Field Reports from February 2022

Missions Conference

February 25, 2022 by Weston in Reports

Yesterday we concluded the mission conference at a certain senior citizen church in the Phoenix valley. It was an amazing and encouraging time.

At Home In Phoenix

February 18, 2022 by Weston in Reports

It is so amazing to finally be back in Phoenix. I always forget what a paradise this place is during the winter. Our last day in Chicago was 40 degrees and raining with ice on the ground. Such a difference!

Packing Up Our Chicago Life

February 11, 2022 by Weston in Reports

This week we have been packing up our house in Chicago and today we are saying goodbye.

This is one of the hardest parts of the missionary life for me. I should have seen this coming but I am not ready to say goodbye. I was not able to prepare myself emotionally for this. But, ready or not, today is the last day.

I will always remember this house fondly. It was only temporary and we have only lived here for one year but I will never forget it. God came and met with us here in this place. Wherever we are God's tabernacling presence follows us. It was especially so in this house. He provided all of our needs and more. He taught us so many hard but beautiful lessons. We spent sweet quality time with Lisa's Dad here. For him this was a house of healing.

Weston and Lisa have been serving in Northeast Thailand for the past eight years among the Isaan people. They are currently leading a team engaged in church planting in the rural countryside.
