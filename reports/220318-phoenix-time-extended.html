Phoenix Time Extended

March 18, 2022 by Weston

Lisa and I have decided to extend out time in Phoenix through May.

Originally we planned to spend the month of May in Chicago doing a special time of prayer, planning and field preparation. But because we have been able to rest so well here in Phoenix we decided to do our pre-field preparation here instead of in Chicago.

This is a big answer to prayer. Thank you for praying for rest! I daresay that this is almost the first time that Lisa and I have felt truly refreshed since we left for the field in 2014.

Weston and Lisa have been serving in Southeath Asia for the past seven years. They are currently leading a team engaged in village church planting.
