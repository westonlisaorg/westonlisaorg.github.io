Field Progress Update

March 11, 2022 by Weston

On Tuesday, the 8th of March, we met with the Global Team from our sending church. This is the main accountability body we report to. This was a very encouraging time for us.

We updated them regarding our last term on the field (2019-2020).

Progress:
Completion of National Language Checks

Lisa completed her national language checks. This enables her to move on to other projects. She is the first person on our team to complete this. I passed my language check in Listening and Reading but I did not pass the Speaking portion.

Village Relationships Established

We developed deep relationships with village leaders in three communities. This is important because our team's mission is village church planting. So, village access is everything.

Strategic Plan Developed

Our team was able to agree on a strategic plan. Our team spent aproximately 6 months in 2020 in discussions hammering out how we will attempt to see new churches planted in our target area.

Problems:
Team Attrition

We saw the attrition of three family units from our team. Adaptation to rural, village life in Bee Creek was very challenging and I think this hit our team pretty hard. As a result of this and other factors we lost 3 units from our team.

Team Disagreement
Covid-19

The global pandemic created a number of challenges for our team. Safety, isolation and the stopping of all activities meant much of our active ministry was put on hold.

Plans:
Complete Final Language Check
Lead Team to Function Well

Weston and Lisa have been serving in Southeath Asia for the past seven years. They are currently leading a team engaged in village church planting.
